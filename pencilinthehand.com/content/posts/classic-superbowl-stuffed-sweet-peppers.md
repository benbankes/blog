date: 2018-02-03T21:43:06-07:00
draft: false
---

Ingredients
- 1 lb **sweet mini peppers**
- 8 oz **cream cheese**
- 2 cups **sharp cheddar cheese**
- 8 oz **chopped ham**
- 1/2 tsp **paprika**
- 1/4 **chopped onion**

Instructions
1. Mix everything except the peppers
1. Cut peppers in half
1. Stuff peppers with stuffing
1. Cook in oven for 10 minutes at 350
